Assumptions and caveats
This is a theoretical calculator based on nozzle diameter, pressure, liquid density, atmospheric pressure, and an empirical correction factor. It is intended for planning and comparison, not as a substitute for experimental validation. Actual collected volume can vary with instrument condition, sheath composition, temperature, stability of the stream, satellite droplets, sort mode, aborts, and collection geometry. Frequency is treated as kHz.
